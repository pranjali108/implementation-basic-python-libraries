Promoting competition: Both laws aim to promote competition and prevent anti-competitive practices. 2. Approach: US Antitrust Law tends to be more permissive, while EU Competition Law is more prescriptive. 2. IPR exhaustion: EU has a more restrictive approach to IPR exhaustion, while the US has a more flexible approach. 3.
